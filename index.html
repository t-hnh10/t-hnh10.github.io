Who am I?

Hi, I'm Tony. I'm currently a student at Coder Academy, working towards a Diploma of Information Technology as I learn how to code.


Following several years of study and employment in the legal industry, I embarked upon a new journey into the tech industry. Computers have always been a topic of interest to me. I first installed Ubuntu 7.04 on the family PC as a teenager, developing a life-long love affair with Linux and free and open-source software. The potential for software is limitless, thus I wish to combine these possibilities with my skills and passion for education, language learning and cultural understanding to effect change in the world that I love.


When I'm not coding, you'll find me on a plane jetting off to my next adventure, trying new foods, or kicking back at home with a textbook learning a new language.

Projects

Pink Compliance

Created by a team of seven at Tanda Hackathon 2019 in a single day, the application connects with Tanda's API to retrieve and present Modern Award wage information in an easy to understand format.


BriskDay

BriskDay strives to be a simple yet beautiful dashboard page. It contains all the relevant information you will need to start off your day; these include the time, latest news headlines provided by The Guardian API and weather from the OpenWeatherMap API.


Guidefully

A tour guide booking service built with Ruby on Rails in 3 days. The application uses Devise for authentication and provides payment functionality provided by Stripe.


CultureFluent

Developed by a team at HealthHack 2019, this is a tool providing patients with future appointment letters in their preferred language. Assists culturally and linguistically diverse (CALD) community members in addressing the current access barriers to health services, improve understanding and communication, as well as engage and work with differing health beliefs, preferences and traditions.


Bookly

A two-sided, student-centric, textbook marketplace. Developed with Ruby on Rails, the web application has built-in authentication, image upload, external database querying, and payment platform.


The Geo Quiz App

A game that provides the user with geography-related questions. It was designed with a scoring function and hard coded questions. The app was completed within the span of several days.
